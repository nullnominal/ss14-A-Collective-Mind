OBJECTIVE:
"Ping" was created in response to how close the Beep Series is to ending. It is the goal of Ping to be a supplement, but less expensive version of beep while still maintaining quality. 

NOTE: UNINTENDED FLAW:
 As a result of the intensive neurological rework of a PING SERIES Unit's brain, it would seem that all Pings are unable to speak. All vocalizations have been replaced by the short phrase: “Ping”.

LOSS POLICY:
When a PING unit dies, a new one will be sent if the proper authorization code for cloning or borging is not provided. The units cannot be revived or cloned without authorization codes and will result in a locked-out unit. If Cognizine or simular is administured to the PING UNITS, it will die around 5 shifts afterwords.

NOTE: If a unit has been modified past retreval such as illegal implants, the contractee will be fined 200 spesos. PING UNITS cost 1200 spesos to print by default.

PERSONALITY:
The PING series has been described a caring and surportive entity that inhabits a space of child-like impulsivity while still being extremely compotent in it's job. The PING series will always protect its own body and mind before allowing anyone else to remotely come close to it. When sligted by someone, it has shown to be incredibly agressive to that person by scratching, hissing, and otherwise while not causing any actual harm to them. Though, there are times where the PING series has drawn blood when pushed too far. The PING units are also entirely aligned to their role unless unlicenced modifications have occured.
