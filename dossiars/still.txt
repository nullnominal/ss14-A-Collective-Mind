[head=2]Crewmember Records[/head]
Crewmember: Still
Date: N/A

Still is an incredibly old shadekin who has total and absolute amnesia because of the experiment that happened in 2130. Though, she is an absolute sweetheart when it comes to things she loves like Mothroaches or her partners. 

Though, Still has PTSD because of something that happened to her on station. Specifically, in 2526, she had called for a death squad for a false revolutionary threat. It has been taken care of through the lovely care of Mollie Moeller though. 

[head=3]Appearance[/head]
Still is an orange shadekin with almost black eyes, and light-blue hair. Her tail and her ears look like they are implanted or got some heavy surgery done on them. She has those scars as the shadekin equivalent of transfeminine procedures on humans. Her height is 136cm, with a weight of 52.6kg.

[head=3]Medical Records[/head]
[head=4]Initial PTSD - Mollie Moller[/head]
It has been documented that Still has PTSD. This happened because of the shift where she called for a death squad but Centcom refused to send it.

At the end of debrief that shift, she tried to throw herself out of an airlock, but it ended up failing as CC locked all of the external doors.

She is ordered to go through checkups atleast every 20 minutes and must have extensive therapy.

She will be very slow to answer any requests and will freak out over seemingly random things. Please be kind to her. To help with this, I am prescribing  10u Psicodine tablets that should be given when she is having a breakdown, or needs to be otherwise available

[head=4]Slight Recovery - Mollie Moller[/head]
I have just heard some great news about Still! She is seemingly starting to cope with her PTSD. I would still recommend giving her the medicine however.

The medicine makes her actually have a personality instead of acting like a borg you can order around. Though, she does still have triggers that will shut even the meds off.

Those triggers include these at this time:
* Being Touched
* Being Dragged

These triggers make her not even respond to borg-like commands unless she is literally programmed to do it in her body. Speaking of that, her body seemingly moves on  its own when she absolutely needs to do something for the station. Though, her eyes are almost entirely blacked out when she is like this, she speaks in a monotone voice. and she works in a way that looks incredibly mechanical.
