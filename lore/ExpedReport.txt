[head=3]0.2 Findings[/head]
Kin traveled into a place we are describing as the
”anti-darkness” world. It contains some weird things
that do not exist in our plane.
When we first went in, it felt like we where being
ripped apart by a lack of something; At the same time,
it felt like our cores where vibrating. It was painful, but
we kept on going.
Thankfully we came out near a seemingly ruined
structure. It was slightly hard to get to though, as we
couldn’t move in this environment. There wasn’t a
ground below us. This structure included something
labled in a language that kin can’t understand, and
when we put both parts on, we stopped being ripped
apart.[bolditalic]
Centcom Addendum: This description seems to imply
that they came out of their dimension into space.[/bolditalic]
When we tried to go to a large circular area, we started
to burn from it. It seems like this is the opposite of
darkness.
After we treated our burns, we decided to check out
another wreck. However, this wreck was different. It
caused our cores to vibrate even more based on how
close we got to it. When we tried to figure out what
was causing our cores to vibrate, we found the blue
crystal. It is harmful and should not be used outside of
extremely controlled experiments.
When we broke the blue crystals in two, it felt like our
core was being broken in two at the same time. Those
can’t be replaced... Kin who where slightly farther away from the crystals said that the closer kin slumped onto the ground for a solid 30 seconds before complaining.
