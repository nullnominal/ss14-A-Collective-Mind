[head=3]Fax Machine Incidents[/head]
On regards to the recent fax machine incidents, these documents seem to be real and are being distributed by an unknown force when the subject known as Mollie Moeller is around. 

Mollie seems to become catatonic for an unknown amount of time that seems variable when reading the documents in marrish or the one note that a "fake" version of her wrote. 

We would assume that she is the current "fake" version, though she is the one we hired. In relation to the Persistence Contract, we have now learned that it is a time fucking anomaly that sends people into the 2140s. Seemingly the suicide note came from that Mollie as ours is still alive.

In regards to our own timeline, Mollie seems to have lived for 500 (give or take 200) years under our care while Moth people are only meant to live up to 60 years.

According to our own internal investigations, Mollie is currently 40 years old to the dot, and this "original" Mollie lost contentiousness when she was 21 years old. After 2130, this fake Mollie took over the original Mollie's body for the years she was employed under us.
