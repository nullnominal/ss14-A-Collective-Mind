[head=3]                                     Objective[/head]
This experiment is being conducted as a way for kin to
explore the outer reaches of what we are calling the
”anti-darkness” realm.
We have tried to send normal bodied kin there before
but they all reported something called ”light” that
caused them to catch fire. To fix this issue, we plan to
use some harmful crystals that they managed to bring
to our home. They resonate with us and hurt badly;
Yet, they also teleport us.
These crystals are going to be used as a way to mind
switch one of our kin into someone else remotely.

[head=3]                                    Procedure[/head]
The kin who was approved to be part of this experiment
was placed in a body-preserving tank filled with
mind-numbing fluid. On the outside of the tank, there
is a console that has a slot for those harmful crystals.
The crystals where then crushed by the machine
causing a massive wave of pressure that partially
destroyed the machines that control the experiment.

[head=3]                                  Observations[/head]
After the crystals where crushed, the kin seemingly
forgot everything about the experiment, and wasn’t
switched with a light-breathing being. However, we
have started to receive weird garbled signals that are
close to the kin hivemind wavelength.
This leads me to believe that there are a multitude of
kin-minded beings out there in the light breathing
world; We just can’t get the data from them.

[head=3]                                     Amendments[/head]
• The kin are abandoning this research and our goal
to get into the light-breathing world entirely. We
are experiencing more and more waves of energy
coming from the spot where we destroyed the
crystals.
• We also partially figured out what the garbled
signals where and where able to partially decode
2
them. It confirms that the subject kin’s mind was
forked into a lot of light-breathers’ minds.
• The kin has been discharged from the area, yet
keeps trying to find the other forks. The kin also
doesn’t seem to even know that the forks exist. We
cannot prompt her to remember with any
technique, and it just seems to actively make it
worse. They are waiting, but also wanting to go out
into the light-breathing world against her own
instincts.
• The subject has also seemingly forgotten their
name, and has dubbed themselves as Still.
