As someone who is going to be working in Science, I [signature]  agree to  follow the following employment contract or be fired at a maximum of three strikes.

If I am a Research Assistant or otherwise new to science, I must go and find the Research Director, an experienced scientist, or one of the rarely active NT Career Trainers before doing anything related to research.

[head=2]Lathes[/head]
• All resource-collection areas must stay above 10 glass, steel, plastic, and plasma for non anomaly or IPC healing related tasks. 
• All item requests that are printed by a protolathe, or a circuit imprinter must have a valid item-request form as directed and stamped by the research director for non-scientists. 

[head=3]Pre-Approved Requests[/head]
• All forms of batteries, with more consideration for IPCs, Borgs, and people with translators.
•  For a cargo technician, or the quartermaster:
    •  A Cargo Telepad
• For Mining and Salvage:
   •  Advanced Resources
   •  Diamond, and normal drills.
   •  Mass Scanners
   •  Material Scanners
•  Engineering: All advanced tools


[head=2]Research[/head]
•  Other than the unlock order, personal projects must be put aside for departmental requests.
•  The research director must approve of any research projects.

[head=3]Unlock Order[/head]
The following is the priority list for unlocking sciences, ordered by priority, and may be unlocked without asking the Research Director.

1. Basic Xenoarchelogy
2. Mechanical Compression
3. Basic Anomalous Research
4. High Capacity Batteries & Hyper Rechargers


 
[head=3]Xenoarchelogy[/head]
• Prefered over all other types of research
• Safety is paramount. If an artifact can do something dangerous, do that node away from the station, and use proper safety gear.
•  Anomaly creating nodes must not be activated before Basic Anomalous Research is completed.

[head=3]Anomalies[/head]
•  Anomalies must not be generated before Basic Anomalous Research is completed
•  All Anomalies must be assigned to a vessel 
•  Anomalies must not be generated without explicit RD approval
•  Upon generating an anomaly, the station must be notified of its existence.

[head=2]Robotics[/head]
[head=3]Mechs[/head]
• Mechs may only be created at request of their specific department head(s)

[head=3]Cyborgs[/head]
• The AIs shunt is the first priority when creating, or maintaining cyborgs.
• Crew members who are gibbed are the second priority.
• New cyborgs must not be created if the basic material count drops below 30 of each
• Cyborg chargers are considered a basic material for cyborgs



[head=3]IPCs[/head]
• An IPC-specific medbay (or wing) must be constructed 
• The IPC medbay must have a stock of at least 100u Oil, 1 stack of HV cables, 10 glass, 10 plasma, and a welding fuel tank.
•  IPCs are not to be unlocked unless it is medically necessary, or they are moving  to a better battery.
• IPCs are to be healed above any non-crew based cyborg.

[head=2]Emergency Situations[/head]
• All non-essential materials are to be given to medical, robotics, security, and engineering.
• Security is the sole determinant of what gets researched
• Any Security member may request any mech
• Anyone can request any non-contraband item from the protolathe

[head=2]Punishment[/head]
1. On the first warning, there will be no fabrication, or resource acquisition.
2. On a second warning, a mentor will be forcefully assigned.
3. On the 3rd warning, access to the science department will be removed.
