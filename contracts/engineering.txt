As someone who is going to be working in Engineering, I [signature]  agree to  follow the following employment contract or be fired at a maximum of three strikes.

If I am a Technical Assistant or otherwise new to Engineering, I must go and find the Chief Engineer, an experienced engineer, or one of the rarely active NCTs before doing anything related to engineering.

[head=2]Engineers[/head]
• A non permanent power generation source must be set up before setting up a permanent engine.
• The power network MUST be checked every 15 minutes
• Technical Assistant related tasks such as teaching MUST be worked on
• AFTER ALL other tasks, personal projects MAY be constructed.

[head=2]Atmospheric Technitions[/head]
• Atmospheric Technicians must set up distribution, and then disposals before moving on to any other tasks
   • failing to do so is grounds for immediate termination.
• Atmospheric Technicians MUST focus on atmospheric repairs before others.
• Atmospheric Technicians SHOULD start making the Thermo Electric Generator.
• Atmospheric Technicians SHOULD make specialized atmospheric conditions for non-human crew members.
• Any mention of "independence" or "Atmosia" is grounds for immediate contract termination.
• Atmospheric Status MUST be checked every 15 minutes. 
• AFTER ALL other tasks, personal projects MAY be constructed.

[head=2]Basic Power[/head]

• The Tesla or Singularity MAY be started without explicit CE approval if either:
  • There is no CE, or
  • The CE refuses to start an engine in any reasonable time
• The Tesla or Singularity must be started before doing any other engineering tasks
• A PRIMARY Engine not being set up before other tasks are done are grounds for immediate dismissal.
• Before starting the Tesla or Singularity, it must be reviewed for any design flaws by the Chief Engineer or any 3rd party Engineer.
• The Singularity SHOULD be used IF there is an advanced engine available because it is easier to decommission. 
• The Singularity and Tesla must be maintained every 15 minutes. 
• On a new PRIMARY engine, the Singularity OR Tesla must be decommissioned. . 
• During an emergency, the engine MUST be constantly secure

[head=2]Repairs[/head]
• Bridge, Evac, Arrivals, and Secure Areas must be prioritized.
• Functionality must be given more concern than aesthetics 
• Engineers may break into areas to provide repairs, provided that:
    • There are no relevant staff around, or
    • The damage is substantial enough to warrant immediate evacuation of the area.

[head=2]Advanced Power[/head]
[bold]Thermo Electric Generator[/bold]
• The TEG Is preferred over all other advanced power sources
• After the TEG makes an extra 150 kilowatts consistently, it is considered the primary source

• The TEG may only be constructed after approval by:
    • an Atmos Technician, or
    • The Chief Engineer

[bold]Nuclear Reactor[/bold]
• A Nuclear Reactor must only be constructed AFTER:
    • Approval by the Chief Engineer,
    • The Captain, and
    • A majority of the remaining command members
• Engineering’s budget must stay above 10k spesos for the reactor to stay online. 
• The Reactor must only be constructed by atmospheric technicians, and the Chief Engineer 
• The Reactor must not be the primary engine
• An Engineer must be stationed nearby at all times. 
• The Reactor may be used to generate money using SMES’, and selling to cargo.
