As someone who is going to be working in Medical, I [signature]  agree to  follow the following employment contract or be fired at a maximum of three strikes.

[head=2]General Practices[/head]
• All chemicals must be in a locker that is access-restricted to medical personnel only
• The chemicals must always be closed, and locked when they are not being actively used
• Patients must be scanned, then the results must be printed, then signed by all who are treating the patient. All printed results must go to the CMO.
• Dead Unrevivable Patients must be given to an applicable surgeon, then stored in a freezer, or an otherwise anti-rot device.
• Using Dexalin (Plus), or Saline on a species of bird is grounds for immediate demotion to a Medical Assistant.
• Likewise using the chemicals meant for bird species on a non bird species is grounds for immediate demotion. 
• Letting a Unique Body Composition patent die intentionally, as interpreted by the CMO, is grounds for disciplinary punishment. 

[head=2]Surgons[/head]
• Dead unique body composition patients should have their brains removed, and then transplanted into a donor body.
• Original bodies of borged patients are to be saved as a donor body in such a manner that no rotting happens.
• Lost body parts are to be re-attached without question
• Elective Surgeries must have a surgical form stamped by the CMO, and signed by the patient.
• Command-level elective surgeries must also be stamped by Officer ‘Blue Shield.’
• Command-level gibbings are to be the first in line for a donor body.

[head=2]Chemists[/head]
• All chemicals must be labeled with the name, effect per unit, overdose count
• If applicable (such as patches, or pills), the chemicals must also have a dose, and the amount it heals.
• Brigmed is a second priority in terms of chemical distribution
• All healing chemicals must stay above 100u 
• Chemical priority should be split up based on the number of chemists. 
• After all basic chemicals are done, chemicals for departments like janitorial, or botany may be produced.

[head=2]Parameds[/head]
• Parameds are to attempt to bring dead patients to a safe place before attempting to treat, or revive, excluding patients Unique Body Composition
• Parameds are to have a selection of basic chemicals on hand at all times

[head=2]Punishment[/head]
1. On the first warning, there will be no chemical usage
2. On a second warning, a mentor will be forcefully assigned.
3. On the 3rd warning, access to the medical department will be removed.
